Current & Past Research

CLAWS: An IRAM 30m Large Programme

Part of my PhD project is to study CO isotopologues in M51 to better understand the physical conditions in the ISM. With the IRAM 30m M51 Large Programme we have unprecedented deep observations in the mm band covering several CO isotopologues simultaneously.

Read More

BASS Project: Restframe NIR of AGN

I am an active member of the BAT AGN Spectroscopic Survey (BASS) project. As part of my Master Thesis I studied the restframe Near Infrared Regime of nearby AGN to probe potential tracers of AGN activities.

Check out Survey Website

MUSE: Probing AGN Unification

I performed active research as part of the MUSE consirtium with Prof. Sebastiano Cantalupo. I studied high Lyα & HeII nebulae around redshift AGN to probe the classical AGN unification theory.
